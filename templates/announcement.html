Who is the real voice behind Indian Railways announcements?

TThe real voices behind Indian Railways announcements are Ranjit Kumar, a former All India Radio announcer known for his distinctive voice, and Usha Uthup, a renowned playback singer who has also recorded announcements for the railway network.

what are the differences between automated and manual modes of announcements?

Indian Railways announcements can be made in two modes:
1. Automated Mode: Through pre-recorded messages played automatically in stations.
2. Manual Mode: By station staff using live microphones for real-time announcements.
