Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book train tickets online?

You can book train tickets online through various travel booking websites and apps. Ensure you have a valid IRCTC ID and payment method.

What documents are required for train travel?

Valid ID proof such as Aadhar card, voter ID, passport, or driving license is required for train travel.

How can I check the train chart?

You can check the train chart 4 hours before the departure from the charting station.

Which is the busiest railway station of India?

Howrah Jn. is the busiest railway station of India followed by New Delhi and Mumbai Central.

Are there any discounts available for senior citizens?

No there are no discounts on Train Tickets for Sr. Citizens but there is reserved lower berth quota for them without discounts.
